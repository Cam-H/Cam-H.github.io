Game Development

I have tried my hand at developing a number of games in a variety of different genres. Most, however, are scrapped before they get anywhere near a presentable state. Nonetheless, I have gained a significant amount of experience simply trying things out on my own, well beyond what I could have following any given curriculum.

For my current project, I have decided to approach development very differently. I have developed a math and ui library. These libraries are designed to be reusable so that I may deploy them in future projects and spend less time remaking old functions. I have recently begun development on the game itself as these two libraries are now up to a usable state. Once I have a working slice, I will begin a devlog. In the meantime, feel free to use my work if you so desire.
